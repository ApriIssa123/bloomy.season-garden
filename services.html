Frequently Asked Questions

Here are quick answers to common questions about hosting events at Bloomy Season Garden.

What types of events can you host?

We host weddings, school trips, birthdays, corporate events, and private parties in our scenic farm setting.

How many guests can Bloomy Season Garden accommodate?

Our outdoor spaces can accommodate small to mid-size gatherings; we typically host up to 300 guests depending on layout.

Do you provide catering and decor services?

Yes, we offer in-house catering and decor packages, with customizable menus and styling to match your theme.

Is parking available on-site?

Yes, ample secure parking is available for guests, with designated drop-off points for ease of access.

How do I book my date?

Contact us to check availability; a deposit secures your date, and our team guides you through planning steps.
